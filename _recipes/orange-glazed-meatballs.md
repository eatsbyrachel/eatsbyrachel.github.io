ingredients:
  - "1 bag Farm Rich Homestyle Meatballs"
  - "1 cup orange marmalade"
  - "1/2 cup rice vinegar"
  - "2 tsp sesame oil"
  - "2 tsp minced garlic"
  - "2 tsp minced ginger"
  - "1 tsp salt"
  - "1 tsp chili pepper flakes (adjust to your heat preference)"
  - "1 sliced scallion"
  - "Pinch of sesame seeds"
instructions:
  - "Cook meatballs according to package directions."
  - "In a saucepan, combine orange marmalade, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, salt, and chili pepper flakes."
  - "Heat until the glaze is warm and well combined."
  - "Toss meatballs in the orange glaze."
  - "Top with sliced scallions and sesame seeds."
  - "Serve as an appetizer and enjoy!"
