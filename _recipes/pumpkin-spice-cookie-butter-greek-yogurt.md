ingredients:
  - "1 container Sprouts pumpkin Greek yogurt (5%)"
  - "1 container plain Fage Greek yogurt (32 oz)"
  - "2-3 tbsp Bonne Maman pumpkin spice spread"
  - "1 jar Lotus Biscoff cookie butter"
  - "6 crushed Lotus Biscoff cookies"
  - "Sprinkle of pumpkin spice"
instructions:
  - "Mix 2-3 tbsp of pumpkin spice spread into the pumpkin Greek yogurt for extra flavor."
  - "Layer pumpkin and plain Greek yogurt alternately in a Greek yogurt strainer."
  - "Add dollops of cookie butter and sprinkle with crushed Biscoff cookies between layers."
  - "Swirl gently with an offset spatula for that marbled effect."
  - "Cover with the pressure plate and spring, then refrigerate for 24 hours."
  - "Drain the whey (save it for smoothies)."
  - "Scoop onto a plate, drizzle with melted cookie butter, and top with more crushed Lotus cookies. Sprinkle with a dash of pumpkin spice on top!"
notes: >-
  I used the Bear Greeks strainer — linked in my Amazon Storefront. The 24-hour strain is essential for the thick texture.

Pumpkin spice meets cookie butter in this creamy, dreamy Greek yogurt that's the perfect autumnal treat!
