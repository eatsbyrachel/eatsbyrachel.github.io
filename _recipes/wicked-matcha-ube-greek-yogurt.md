ingredients:

  - "8g matcha powder + 60ml hot water + splash of agave"
  - "8g purple sweet potato powder + 60ml hot water + splash of agave"
instructions:
  - "Sift matcha into a bowl, add hot water, whisk, and sweeten with agave."
  - "Repeat with purple sweet potato powder."
  - "Fold the matcha mixture into one yogurt container."
  - "Fold the purple mixture into the second container."
  - "Alternate each yogurt flavor in a Greek yogurt strainer."
  - "Cover with the pressure plate and spring, then refrigerate for at least 24 hours."
  - "Drain the whey (save it for smoothies)."
  - "Scoop, plate, and top with your favorites!"
notes: >-
  I used the Bear Greeks strainer. The 24-hour strain time is key for the thick, scoopable texture.
