ingredients:
  - "2 ripe white peaches"
  - "7 oz condensed milk"
  - "1 cup heavy cream"
  - "1 bag white chocolate candy wafers"
  - "1-2 tbsp neutral oil"
  - "1 tbsp red dry food coloring powder"
instructions:
  - "Blend the white peach into a smooth puree."
  - "Mix the peach puree with condensed milk."
  - "Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form, then fold into the peach mixture."
  - "Melt white chocolate candy wafers with oil, adjusting consistency by adding more oil if needed."
  - "Spoon into peach molds and ensure the interior is well coated with chocolate."
  - "Rest in the fridge for 30 minutes or until chocolate is set."
  - "Spoon peach ice cream mixture into molds and then add popsicle sticks."
  - "Pipe white chocolate over the top of the mold and around the popsicle stick."
  - "Freeze overnight."
  - "The next day, remove from the molds carefully."
  - "Dab red food coloring powder to give it that peach skin effect."
notes: >-
  Inspired by @ms_shi_mr_he. You'll need a peach-shaped silicone mold and popsicle sticks. You can also use blended freeze-dried strawberry powder instead of food coloring.
