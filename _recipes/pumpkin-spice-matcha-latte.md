ingredients:
  - "-- Pumpkin Cold Foam --"
  - "2-3 tbsp heavy cream"
  - "1 tsp pumpkin spice spread (Bonne Maman)"
  - "-- Matcha Latte --"
  - "4g matcha powder"
  - "40ml hot water"
  - "120ml milk of choice"
  - "1 tbsp pumpkin spice spread (Bonne Maman)"
  - "-- Toppings --"
  - "Sprinkle of pumpkin spice"
  - "1-2 Lotus Biscoff cookies"
instructions:
  - "To make the cold foam, whisk together heavy cream and pumpkin spice spread until soft peaks form."
  - "Sift matcha powder in a bowl."
  - "Add hot water and whisk until frothy."
  - "In a glass cup, add a layer of pumpkin spice spread."
  - "Add ice, milk, and top with matcha mixture."
  - "Add pumpkin cold foam on top."
  - "Sprinkle with additional pumpkin spice (optional)!"
