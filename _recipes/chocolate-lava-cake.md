ingredients:
  - "4 oz semi-sweet chocolate, chopped"
  - "1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter"
  - "1 cup powdered sugar"
  - "2 large eggs"
  - "2 large egg yolks"
  - "6 tablespoons all-purpose flour"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla extract"
  - "Pinch of salt"
  - "Butter and cocoa powder for ramekins"
  - "Vanilla ice cream or whipped cream for serving"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Butter four 6-oz ramekins and dust with cocoa powder. Place on a baking sheet."
  - "Melt chocolate and butter together in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second intervals, stirring between each, until smooth."
  - "Stir in powdered sugar until well combined."
  - "Whisk in eggs and egg yolks, then add vanilla extract."
  - "Fold in flour and salt until just combined. Do not overmix."
  - "Divide batter evenly among the prepared ramekins."
  - "Bake for exactly 12-14 minutes. The edges should be firm but the center will be soft."
  - "Let cool for 1 minute, then run a knife around the edges. Invert onto plates."
  - "Serve immediately with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream."

If you want to impress someone, make these Chocolate Lava Cakes. They look like you spent hours in the kitchen, but the secret is they only take about 15 minutes to prep.

The moment you cut into one and that warm, gooey chocolate center flows out... pure magic. I make these every Valentine's Day and they never disappoint!
