ingredients:
  - "1 package Trader Joe's Pumpkins Sugar Cookie Dough"
  - "1 cup melted butter (browned)"
  - "1 cup brown sugar"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "1 tsp vanilla"
  - "2/3 cup pumpkin puree (strained)"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "1 tsp baking soda"
  - "1/2 tsp salt"
  - "1 tsp pumpkin pie spice"
  - "1 cup dark chocolate chips"
instructions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 350°F."
  - "Strain pumpkin puree with a paper towel. Squeeze until moisture is removed."
  - "In a bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, salt, and pumpkin pie spice."
  - "In a separate bowl, whisk together brown sugar, sugar, and brown butter."
  - "Add egg yolks, vanilla, and pumpkin puree."
  - "Fold in dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix."
  - "Add chocolate chips and continue folding until just combined."
  - "Scoop ~2 tbsp cookie dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper."
  - "Bake for 8 minutes. Remove from the oven."
  - "Press Trader Joe's Pumpkins Sugar Cookie Dough into the center of each cookie."
  - "Bake for another 10 minutes."
  - "Let the cookies cool for a few minutes before digging in!"
notes: >-
  Pumpkin chocolate chip cookie recipe by @butternutbakery. Make sure to strain the pumpkin puree well for the best texture.

Level up Trader Joe's Pumpkins Sugar Cookie Dough by making it actually pumpkin flavored! These double decker cookies feature a brown butter pumpkin chocolate chip base topped with TJ's iconic pumpkin cookie dough.
