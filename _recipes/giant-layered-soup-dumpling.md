ingredients:
  - "1/2 lb ground pork"
  - "1 sliced green onion"
  - "1 tsp mushroom umami powder"
  - "1 tsp brown sugar"
  - "1 tbsp cooking wine"
  - "1 tsp soy sauce"
  - "1 tsp sesame oil"
  - "2 tbsp minced ginger"
  - "1 tbsp water"
  - "2 tsp oyster sauce"
  - "1/3 cup water"
  - "6-8 wonton wrappers"
  - "2 napa cabbage leaves"
  - "1 tsp chili oil"
instructions:
  - "Combine ground pork with all seasonings and mix well."
  - "In a small claypot, layer seasoned pork, napa cabbage, and 2 wonton wrapper sheets."
  - "Repeat until the claypot is nearly full."
  - "Pour 1/3 cup water over the top layer of pork."
  - "Add the final wonton wrapper layer."
  - "Steam for 20 minutes."
  - "Drizzle with chili oil, top with sliced green onions, and enjoy!"
notes: >-

Why fold dozens of soup dumplings when you can make one giant, layered one? This clever claypot method gives you all the flavor of soup dumplings with a fraction of the effort.
