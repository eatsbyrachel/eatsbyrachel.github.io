ingredients:
  - "300g whole milk"
  - "20g fine sugar"
  - "30g tapioca starch"
  - "1 tsp ube flavoring"
  - "1/4 cup mini mochi balls"
  - "2 scoops passion fruit mango ice cream"
  - "Colored sprinkles or candies"
instructions:
  - "Prepare toppings."
  - "Combine milk, sugar, tapioca starch, and ube flavoring in a saucepan over medium heat."
  - "Stir constantly until the mixture starts to thicken."
  - "Remove from heat, add toppings, and enjoy!"

My childhood dream of eating slime just came true! This ube milk mochi is warm, stretchy, and absolutely dreamy — topped with ice cream, mochi balls, and sprinkles.
