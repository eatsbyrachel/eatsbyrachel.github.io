ingredients:
  - "4g hojicha powder"
  - "40ml hot water"
  - "120ml milk of choice"
  - "2 tbsp heavy cream"
  - "4 tsp banana syrup (homemade: 1 cup water, 1/2 cup sugar, 1 ripe banana boiled 10 min then pureed)"
  - "Sprinkle of crushed shortbread cookies"
instructions:
  - "Sift hojicha powder into a bowl."
  - "Add hot water and whisk. Set aside."
  - "Prepare cream top by combining 2 tsp of banana syrup with 2 tbsp of heavy cream."
  - "Using an electric whisk, mix until soft peaks form in the cream."
  - "Add 2 tsp of banana syrup into the bottom of a glass."
  - "Add a layer of ice."
  - "Add 120ml milk of choice."
  - "Pour hojicha mixture over the milk."
  - "Pour cream mixture over the top."
  - "Finish with crushed shortbread cookies and sliced bananas!"
notes: >-
  Homemade banana syrup: combine 1 cup water, 1/2 cup sugar, and 1 ripe banana in a saucepan and boil for 10 minutes. Puree using a blender until smooth. Use code RACHEL10 for 10% off at Chikiriya Tea House.
