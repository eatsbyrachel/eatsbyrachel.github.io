ingredients:
  - "1/2 gallon melted ice cream (any flavor — ube works great!)"
  - "1 loaf sliced brioche bread"
  - "A few tbsp butter"
  - "Powdered sugar (optional)"
  - "Fresh berries (optional)"
  - "Maple syrup (optional)"
instructions:
  - "Let the ice cream melt at room temp for a few hours or in the fridge overnight. (Microwave on defrost works too!)"
  - "Heat a tablespoon of butter in a pan over medium-low."
  - "Dip each bread slice quickly into the melted ice cream and transfer to the pan right away. If the bread soaks too long, it becomes too soggy to flip."
  - "Cook until golden brown on both sides."
  - "Serve warm with toppings of your choice!"
notes: >-
  Inspired by @lorafied. The key is to dip quickly — don't let the bread soak!
---

Sink your teeth into this super simple French toast hack that requires only 2 ingredients! Melted ice cream replaces the traditional egg mixture for an incredibly flavorful twist.
