ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup water"
  - "1/4 cup evaporated milk"
  - "1/4 cup fine granulated sugar"
  - "2 whole eggs + 2 yolks"
  - "1 tsp vanilla extract"
  - "Pinch of salt"
  - "1 package frozen egg tart shells (from your local Asian market)"
instructions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 400°F."
  - "Place frozen egg tart shells on a baking sheet."
  - "In a large bowl, whisk together water, milk, sugar, eggs, egg yolks, vanilla extract, and salt."
  - "Strain the mixture through a sieve."
  - "Pour into egg tart shells."
  - "Bake for 15-20 minutes just until the custard is set and no longer jiggles in the center."
