ingredients:
  - "4 cloves garlic, minced"
  - "1 onion, diced"
  - "3 spicy Italian sausages"
  - "1/2 jar tomato sauce"
  - "3 cups chicken broth"
  - "1/2 cup heavy cream"
  - "1/2 box lasagna noodles, crushed"
  - "1/2 cup mozzarella"
  - "1/4 cup parmesan"
  - "1/4 cup basil"
instructions:
  - "Heat oil in a large pot. Sauté garlic and onion until soft and lightly golden."
  - "Add sausage, breaking it up, and cook until browned."
  - "Stir in tomato sauce, chicken broth, and heavy cream."
  - "Add crushed lasagna noodles and bring to a boil. Cook about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally."
  - "Lower heat and stir in mozzarella, parmesan, and basil until melty and creamy."
  - "Bring to a gentle final boil, then turn off heat."
  - "Finish with extra parmesan and basil if you're feeling it."
