ingredients:
  - "2 tubs (32 oz) Fage 5% Greek yogurt"
  - "1/4 cup freeze-dried raspberry powder"
  - "1/2 cup raspberry coulis"
  - "1/4 cup cocoa powder"
  - "1/2 cup melted dark chocolate"
  - "1-2 tsp neutral oil"
instructions:
  - "Divide yogurt into 3 bowls."
  - "Mix raspberry coulis and powder into one bowl."
  - "Mix cocoa powder into another."
  - "Leave the last bowl plain."
  - "Layer all 3 flavors evenly in a yogurt strainer, flatten the top, and add the pressure plate and spring. Chill 24 hrs."
  - "Drain the whey (save it for smoothies)."
  - "Scoop onto a plate, drizzle with melted chocolate and raspberry coulis, and sprinkle with raspberry powder."
  - "Enjoy!"
notes: >-
  I used the strainer from Bear Greeks. The 24-hour strain is essential for that thick, scoopable texture.

A gorgeous three-layer Greek yogurt featuring raspberry, chocolate, and plain — strained to thick perfection and drizzled with melted dark chocolate and raspberry coulis.
