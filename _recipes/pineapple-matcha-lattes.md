ingredients:
  - "-- Pineapples and Cream Matcha Latte --"
  - "1 can Dole Pineapple Chunks"
  - "4g matcha"
  - "40 ml hot water"
  - "120 ml milk of choice"
  - "45 ml heavy cream"
  - "-- Sparkling Pineapple Drink --"
  - "1 can Dole 100% Pineapple Juice"
  - "1 can sparkling water"
  - "4g matcha"
  - "40 ml hot water"
instructions:
  - "Pineapples and Cream Matcha Latte: Scoop 2 tsp pineapple juice from the can and combine with heavy cream. Whip until lightly thickened."
  - "Sift matcha into a bowl, add hot water, and whisk until frothy."
  - "Add a few pineapple chunks to a cup and gently mash."
  - "Fill with ice, pour in milk and matcha, then finish with pineapple cold foam."
  - "Sparkling Pineapple Drink: Sift matcha into a bowl, add hot water, and whisk until frothy."
  - "Fill a glass with ice."
  - "Add pineapple juice, then sparkling water."
  - "Top with matcha and gently stir."
notes: >-
  Two different takes on pineapple matcha — one creamy, one bubbly. Both are refreshing and perfect for warm weather.
---

Sip up some sunshine by adding pineapple to your matcha lattes! Whether you're looking for a creamy concoction or a bright, bubbly drink, these 2 recipes have you covered.
