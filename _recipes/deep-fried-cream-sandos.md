ingredients:
  - "1 loaf sliced Japanese milk bread"
  - "1 1/2 cups vegetable oil"
  - "1 cup heavy cream"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1 tsp vanilla extract"
  - "4 strawberries, sliced"
  - "2 tsp matcha powder"
  - "3 drops ube extract"
  - "Sweet potato powder for dusting"
  - "Matcha powder for dusting"
instructions:
  - "Fry milk bread in hot oil until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels."
  - "Slice diagonally, then carefully carve out the center."
  - "Whip heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla to stiff peaks. Divide into 3 bowls. Fold 2 tsp matcha into one bowl and 3 drops of ube extract into another."
  - "Stuff each slice of bread with cream using a spatula."
  - "Top plain cream with strawberries."
  - "Dust matcha cream with matcha powder."
  - "Dust ube cream with sweet potato powder."
  - "Enjoy and try not to eat them all at once!"

These viral Japanese-inspired deep fried cream sandos feature crispy golden milk bread stuffed with fluffy whipped cream in three incredible flavors: vanilla strawberry, matcha, and ube.
