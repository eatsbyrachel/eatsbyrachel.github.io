  - "Scoop ice cream on top of one cookie."
  - "Sandwich remaining cookie on top and fill in edges with additional ice cream."
  - "Gently roll in a dish filled with Rice Krispie cereal until coated on all sides."
  - "Freeze for 2 hours or until ice cream is set."

Costco's bakery knows what's up! These giant ice cream sandwiches use their marshmallow crispy cookies as the base, filled with vanilla ice cream and coated in crunchy Rice Krispies.
