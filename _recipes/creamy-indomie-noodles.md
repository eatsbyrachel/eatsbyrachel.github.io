ingredients:
  - "2 packs Indomie noodles"
  - "1 shallot, finely chopped"
  - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
  - "1 tbsp tomato paste"
  - "2 tbsp cream cheese"
  - "2 cups boiling hot water"
  - "Sliced green onions"
  - "Sesame seeds"
instructions:
  - "Sauté garlic and shallots in oil until fragrant."
  - "Stir in tomato paste, then add the Indomie seasoning packets."
  - "Add 1/4 cup hot water and mix until a thick sauce forms."
  - "Stir in cream cheese until smooth and creamy."
  - "Add boiling water, then noodles."
  - "Cook until noodles are tender and sauce is ultra creamy."
  - "Finish with green onions and sesame seeds."
