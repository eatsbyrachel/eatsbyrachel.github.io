ingredients:
  - "6 all-butter frozen croissants (Au Pain Doré from Costco)"
  - "1/2 cup melted butter"
  - "1/2 cup brown sugar"
  - "4 tbsp honey"
  - "Pinch of salt"
instructions:
  - "Bake croissants according to package instructions."
  - "Whisk together melted butter, brown sugar, honey, and salt."
  - "Slice croissants in half and dip each piece into the mixture, brushing to coat evenly."
  - "Bake at 350°F for 15-20 minutes until golden and caramelized."
  - "Let cool until the coating hardens. Enjoy that sweet crunch!"
