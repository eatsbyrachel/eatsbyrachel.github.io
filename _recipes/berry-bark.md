ingredients:
  - "1/4 cup blackberries"
  - "3/4 cup blueberries"
  - "1/2 cup Ghirardelli dark chocolate wafers (melted)"
  - "1/2 cup Ghirardelli white chocolate wafers (melted)"
  - "1 1/2 tsp matcha powder"
  - "Pinch of flaky sea salt"
instructions:
  - "Rinse and pat berries completely dry."
  - "Melt white chocolate, sift in matcha, and mix until smooth."
  - "Melt dark chocolate separately."
  - "Arrange berries on a parchment lined baking sheet."
  - "Drizzle matcha chocolate, then dark chocolate over berries."
  - "Sprinkle with flaky sea salt."
  - "Chill in the fridge 2-3 hrs, then snap and snack!"
