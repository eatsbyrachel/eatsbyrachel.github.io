ingredients:
  - "1 can crescent rolls"
  - "1 block cream cheese"
  - "1 jar guava fruit spread"
  - "Heavy cream for brushing (optional)"
  - "Demerara sugar for sprinkling (optional)"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 375°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper."
  - "Separate crescent roll dough into individual triangles."
  - "Add a small cube of cream cheese to each triangle."
  - "Top with about 1 teaspoon guava fruit spread."
  - "Brush with heavy cream and sprinkle with demerara sugar if using."
  - "Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown."

These 3-ingredient guava cream cheese crescent rolls are dangerously easy and made with just a few Trader Joe's staples. Low effort baking, high reward. They come out creamy, lightly sweet, and perfectly tangy from the guava spread.
