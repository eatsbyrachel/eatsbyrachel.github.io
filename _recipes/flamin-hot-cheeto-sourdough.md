servings: 8
difficulty: hard
ingredients:
  - "150g sourdough starter (100% hydration)"
  - "300g warm water"
  - "25g olive oil"
  - "500g bread flour"
  - "100g pulverized Flamin' Hot Cheetos"
  - "130g chopped Flamin' Hot Cheetos"
  - "10g salt"
instructions:
  - "Mix sourdough starter, warm water, and olive oil together."
  - "Add bread flour and pulverized Cheetos. Mix until combined."
  - "Add salt and mix until incorporated."
  - "Fold in chopped Cheetos."
  - "Bulk ferment with stretch and folds every 30 minutes for 4 hours."
  - "Shape the dough and place in a banneton."
  - "Cold proof in the fridge overnight."
  - "Bake in a Dutch oven at 450°F — 20 minutes covered, 20-25 minutes uncovered."
notes: >-
  Inspired by @theclevercarrot and @mamalydx4. You'll need an active sourdough starter for this recipe.
