  - "1 1/2 cups fresh raspberries"
  - "1 tbsp powdered sugar"
instructions:
  - "Scrape off the top layer of frosting on one cake."
  - "Spread a thin layer of raspberry jam."
  - "Stack the second cake on top."
  - "Smooth out the frosting and coat the sides."
  - "Top with fresh raspberries and a dusting of powdered sugar."
  - "Slice, serve, and enjoy!"
