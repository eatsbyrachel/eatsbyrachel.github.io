ingredients:
  - "2 sticks butter (at room temperature)"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1/2 cup powdered sugar"
  - "1 tsp vanilla extract OR peppermint extract"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "Pinch of salt"
  - "1 tsp gel red food coloring"
instructions:
  - "In a large bowl, cream together butter, sugar, powdered sugar, and extract until smooth."
  - "Mix in the egg until combined."
  - "Add flour and salt. Mix until a shaggy dough forms."
  - "Use your hands to shape dough into a large ball."
  - "Divide dough into 2 equal parts."
  - "Add red gel food coloring into one portion and mix well."
  - "Grab 2 small balls of each colored dough (~2 tsp) and roll into logs."
  - "Pinch the top of the dough together and twist so the colors alternate."
  - "Flatten twisted dough and gently curve the top to form a candy cane shape."
  - "Bake at 350°F for 10-15 minutes. Enjoy!"
