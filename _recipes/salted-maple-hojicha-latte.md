ingredients:
  - "4g hojicha powder"
  - "40g hot water"
  - "1 tsp maple butter (Trader Joe's)"
  - "120ml oat milk"
  - "1 can salted maple cold foam creamer (Trader Joe's)"
instructions:
  - "Sift hojicha powder into a bowl."
  - "Whisk with hot water until smooth."
  - "Add maple butter and whisk until combined."
  - "Pour ice and oat milk into a glass, then top with the hojicha mixture."
  - "Finish with a generous layer of salted maple cold foam and a sprinkle of hojicha powder."
notes: >-
  Both the maple butter and salted maple cold foam creamer are available at Trader Joe's.

The coziest way to welcome fall — a salted maple hojicha latte. The roasted, nutty hojicha paired with maple butter and salted maple cold foam is pure autumn bliss.
