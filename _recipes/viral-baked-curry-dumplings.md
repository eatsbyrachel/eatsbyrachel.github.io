ingredients:
  - "1/4 cup soyaki (from Trader Joe's)"
  - "1/2 bottle Thai red curry sauce (from Trader Joe's)"
  - "1 can coconut cream or coconut milk"
  - "1/4 cup water"
  - "1 package frozen dumplings"
  - "1/4 cup chopped bok choy"
  - "Sesame seeds"
  - "Sliced green onions"
  - "Chili oil"
instructions:
  - "Whisk together soyaki, curry sauce, coconut cream, and water."
  - "Place frozen dumplings in an 8x8 pan and pour sauce over."
  - "Sprinkle with bok choy, cover with foil, and bake at 400°F for 25-30 min."
  - "Remove foil, top with sesame seeds, green onions, and chili oil."
  - "Serve with rice and enjoy!"
