ingredients:
  - "1 tbsp minced garlic"
  - "1 tbsp sliced green onions"
  - "1 tbsp spicy bean paste (doubanjiang)"
  - "4 oz ground pork"
  - "Splash of Shaoxing wine"
  - "Splash of black vinegar"
  - "1 package frozen udon noodles"
  - "1 silken tofu"
instructions:
  - "In an oiled pan over medium heat, sauté garlic and green onions."
  - "Add spicy bean paste and stir."
  - "Add ground pork and cook, breaking it into small bits."
  - "Add a splash of Shaoxing wine and black vinegar."
  - "Boil frozen udon until just tender, then drain."
  - "Blend silken tofu until smooth and creamy."
  - "Assemble: noodles, pork, and tofu."
  - "Mix well and enjoy!"
notes: >-

Who knew silken tofu could make noodles THIS creamy? This mapo tofu-inspired udon dish uses blended silken tofu for an unbelievably smooth, creamy sauce.
