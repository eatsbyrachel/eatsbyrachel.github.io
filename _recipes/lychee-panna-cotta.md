servings: 4
difficulty: medium
ingredients:
  - "1 lb lychee (peeled and pitted)"
  - "1 cup whole milk"
  - "1 cup heavy cream"
  - "1/4 cup water"
  - "1/3 cup white sugar"
  - "1 pack gelatin (7g)"
  - "4 Laughing Cow cheese slices"
instructions:
  - "Peel and remove the pit of around 12 fresh lychee and place into a container."
  - "In a small bowl, combine gelatin with water. Mix and let bloom."
  - "Over low heat, combine milk and sugar. Stir until dissolved."
  - "In a bowl, add 1 slice of Laughing Cow Cheese. Pour a splash of the milk mixture over and whisk to break apart clumps."
  - "Add remaining 3 slices and more milk mixture. Whisk until smooth."
  - "Strain the mixture back into the saucepan."
  - "Add heavy cream and gelatin, whisk until combined."
  - "Pour into the container lined with lychee."
  - "Refrigerate overnight."
  - "The next day, dig in and enjoy!"
notes: >-
  Inspired by @lifewithtuyen and @twaydabae. The Laughing Cow cheese adds extra creaminess to the panna cotta.
---

Live, laugh, love lychee! This panna cotta is creamy, delicate, and studded with fresh lychee. The secret ingredient? Laughing Cow cheese for extra creaminess.
