ingredients:
  - "-- Matcha Latte --"
  - "4g matcha powder"
  - "40ml hot water"
  - "120ml milk of choice"
  - "-- Pistachio Cold Foam --"
  - "2 tbsp heavy cream"
  - "1 tbsp milk of choice"
  - "2 tsp Trader Joe's pistachio spread"
  - "Sprinkle of crushed pistachios"
instructions:
  - "Whisk together heavy cream, milk, and pistachio spread until slightly thick in texture."
  - "Sift matcha and whisk with hot water until frothy."
  - "Add ice and milk over the spread, then pour in your matcha."
  - "Top with pistachio cold foam."
  - "Sprinkle with crushed pistachios."
