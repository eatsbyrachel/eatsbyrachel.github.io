ingredients:
  - "1 box Trader Joe's Ube Mochi Pancake & Waffle Mix"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/4 cup neutral oil"
  - "1 cup coconut milk"
  - "1 tsp ube flavoring"
instructions:
  - "Preheat the oven to 400°F."
  - "Whisk together eggs, sugar, oil, coconut milk, and ube flavoring in a large bowl."
  - "Add in the Trader Joe's ube mix and whisk until smooth."
  - "Scoop batter into a greased muffin tin, filling each about 3/4 of the way."
  - "Bake for ~20 mins or until lightly golden."
  - "Let cool slightly and enjoy the chewy magic!"

Who said Trader Joe's Ube Mochi Pancake & Waffle Mix has to be for pancakes or waffles? Let's turn it into soft, chewy ube mochi muffins instead. Bonus: They're also gluten free!
